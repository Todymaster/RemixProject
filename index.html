The AI Scenario

Imagine asking an AI to build a website. It happens instantly. Does this mean the end of the human programmer?

Headlines scream about layoffs at tech giants. But experts argue this is cost-cutting, not just AI. The reality is nuanced: AI is a powerful engine, but it needs a driver.

Key Insight: AI generates code, but humans generate value.

Why AI Fails: The Bug Problem

AI lacks "common sense." It hallucinates solutions that look right but fail to run. It introduces bugs that are hard to find.
